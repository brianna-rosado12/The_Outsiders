The Outsiders

The Outsiders is a novel that was written in 1967 by Author Susan E. Hinton

"The Outsiders " is a novel about a 14 year old boy nameds Ponyboy and his struggles with fitting into a society where he feels like an outsider.

In the novel we are presented with two rival gangs the "Greasers" and the "Socs". The Greasers are what society refers to as the poor east-side kids.
Soc or Socials are rich west-side kids.
This story not only follows the conflict between these two groups but the way this life-style affects our main character Ponyboy.
